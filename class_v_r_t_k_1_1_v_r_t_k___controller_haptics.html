The TriggerHapticPulse/4 method calls a haptic pulse for a specified amount of time rather than just a single tick.

Each pulse can be separated by providing a pulseInterval to pause between each haptic pulse.

Parameters
controllerReferenceThe reference to the controller to activate the haptic feedback on.
strengthThe intensity of the rumble of the controller motor. 0 to 1.
durationThe length of time the rumble should continue for.
pulseIntervalThe interval to wait between each haptic pulse.
