frequencyThe frequency of the first octave
lacunarityThe frequency multiplier between successive octaves. Experiment between 1.5 - 3.5.
octavesThe amount of detail in the fractal - more octaves mean more detail and longer calc time.
persistanceThe roughness of the fractal noise. Controls how quickly amplitudes diminish for successive octaves. 0..1.
